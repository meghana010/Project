Health is Wealth

Physical activities: Encouraging physical activities such as running, playing games, or sports can help improve the children's physical fitness, balance, and coordination. Organizing activities like outdoor games, dancing, or yoga can be beneficial.

Health education:Educating children about hygiene, healthy eating, and good health practices can help them understand how to take care of their bodies.

Medical check-ups: Regular medical check-ups can help identify any health problems the children might have and treat them before they become serious.

Nutritious meals: Providing nutritious meals is important for children's health. It is essential to ensure that the children receive balanced meals that meet their nutritional requirements.

Emotional support: Providing emotional support and counseling to children can help them deal with the trauma of losing their families and the challenges of living in an orphanage.
