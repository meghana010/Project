Guidelines for E-Adoption of children in Yashoda:

  • After you get into our site's Home page,you can look for the organizations.
  • Our website displays a list of organizations,if you want to know more about the particular organization,you can
    • Click on more details to view more
    • Click on Click here to adopt to adopt a child
    • Then you are directed to a Profiles page which contails the profiles of all children with all the required details.
    • The profile of each child contains the following details:
      • Name:
      • Age:
      • Child Id:(which is unique for every child)
      • Mobile No.:
  • If you want to adopt a child click on adopt/withdraw button.
  • Then you are directed to a registration form.
  • The person who wants to adopt has to enter the id of the child whom he/she wants to adopt in place of childId and also fill the required details.
  • Then click on submit.
  • The person is registered as the adoptee now.
  • The adoptee now can take care of the needs of the foster child.
  • The concept of this E-adoption is that it can be temporary or permanent,the adoptee can withdraw at any time.
  • If at any point of time,the adoptee wants to withdraw as an adoptee he/she can click on Adopt/withdraw button again.
  • The adoptee has to fill the correct details as he/she entered before,so as to identify he/she is the same person.
  • Then he/she is withdrawn as the adoptee from the database.
