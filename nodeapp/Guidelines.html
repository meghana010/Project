Guidelines for E-Adoption of children in Yashoda:

  • This adoption process,called as E-Adoption,by its name is completely Virtual.
  • There is no involvement of legal terms and legal processes.
  • The person who wants to go for E-Adoption has to fill the registration form provided,and if he/she is a authorized person he can proceed further.
  • The adoptee is completely free to withdraw when he is not willing to or not in a condition to contribute for the child's needs.
  • The foster parents need to maintain the records of their contributions to the children and check that the money reaches the intended child.
  • The adoptees have the complete right to know all the information about the childs from the agency or orphanage heads.
  • The foster parents according to their will can be emotionally connected to the children,but they cannot meet directly without the permission of the orphanage heads.
  • Incase if any parent wants to meet the child,they have to undergo the background checks conducted by the agency.
  • The process may involve interviews, background checks, home visits, and discussions on parenting skills, and ability to provide a safe and nurturing environment for the child.
