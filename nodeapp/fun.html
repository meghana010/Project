Do anything,but don't forget to have joy!!

Creative activities: Activities such as painting, music, and drama can help children relax, express themselves, and improve their mental health.

Gardening: Gardening is a great way to introduce children to healthy eating habits and teach them about where their food comes from. It also provides physical activity and exposure to nature.

Group outings: Taking children on outings to parks, museums, and other outdoor activities can provide them with opportunities to exercise, explore, and learn.

Skill building: Encouraging skill-building activities such as reading, writing, and computer literacy can help children improve their cognitive abilities and overall health.

Cooking Classes: Arrange cooking classes where children can learn how to prepare simple, healthy meals or snacks

Talent Show: Organize a talent show where children can showcase their talents and abilities. This can include singing, dancing, poetry recitation, or acting.

Sports Day: Organize a sports day, where children can play various sports, such as football, basketball, volleyball, or cricket.
